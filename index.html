Questions people ask before getting in touch

What happens after I send the form?

I reply within 24 hours — usually the same day — with a couple of questions about your business. If you've asked for a free mockup, I'll build a preview of your homepage and send you a link within a few days. Then it's entirely up to you.

Is the mockup really free? What's the catch?

Really free, no catch. I build it, you look at it, and if it's not for you, you keep the design with my compliments and never hear from me again. I do it because showing you is easier than describing it.

I already have a website — can you still help?

Yes. I can fix a slow or outdated site, sort out your Google ranking, or add things like online booking to what you already have. You don't need to start from scratch to work with me.

Who owns the website once it's built?

You do. The domain is registered in your name and the site is yours. The monthly care plan covers hosting and updates, but if you ever want to leave, I hand everything over — no lock-in, no ransom.

How quickly can my site go live?

A Starter site is live 5–7 days after I have your content (photos, opening hours, prices). Growth sites take 10–14 days, and Pro builds 2–3 weeks. I'll give you an exact date before we start.

Do you only work with businesses near Bishop's Stortford?

Local businesses in Hertfordshire, Essex and East London are my focus, but everything can be done remotely by phone and email — so if you're elsewhere in the UK, get in touch anyway.
