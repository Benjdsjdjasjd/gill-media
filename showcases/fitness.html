About Me

NOT JUST A TRAINER.
A PARTNER IN YOUR GOALS.

I'm Jake Morrison, a Level 3 qualified personal trainer based in St Albans, Hertfordshire. After spending over a decade in competitive sports and transforming my own physique, I made it my mission to help others unlock their potential.

I don't believe in cookie-cutter programmes or unsustainable crash diets. Every client gets a science-backed, individually tailored plan that fits their life — not the other way around. Whether you want to lose weight, build muscle, or simply feel stronger and more confident, I'll get you there.
