Benvenuti

At Bella Tavola, every dish tells the story of la cucina italiana — handed down through generations and reimagined with the finest seasonal produce. From hand-rolled pasta to wood-fired pizzas, our kitchen honours the traditions of Italy while celebrating the vibrant flavours of Hertfordshire's local suppliers. Join us for an evening you won't forget.
