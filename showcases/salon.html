Our Story

A Salon Built on Passion

Nestled in the heart of Bishop’s Stortford, Maison Hair Studio has been a sanctuary for those who believe beautiful hair is an art form. Founded over twelve years ago, we’ve grown from a small studio into one of Hertfordshire’s most trusted salons.

Our team of five experienced stylists brings together decades of combined expertise, a genuine love for their craft, and an unwavering commitment to making every client feel extraordinary. We use only the finest products and stay at the forefront of technique and trend.

At Maison, it’s not just about hair — it’s about the feeling you carry with you when you leave.
