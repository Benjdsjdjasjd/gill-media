Our Programmes

Whether you're picking up a basketball for the first time or competing at squad level, there's a place for you.

Community Sessions

Ages 7–14. Skills, footwork, ball-handling, shooting, and small-sided games. No experience required — just turn up and play.

      Coaching Team

      Led by experienced coaches with a passion for developing the next generation of basketball players.

      Head Coach & Founder

      Phil Molyneux

      Former National League player with international experience playing and coaching in America and Hong Kong. Phil holds a degree in coaching and performance development plus a master's in sports business management. A Year 5 teacher at St Edmund's prep school and father of three, Phil founded the Vikings in November 2021 to bring basketball to Bishop's Stortford.
